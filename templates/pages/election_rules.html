Election Rules

Guidelines for a fair and transparent election.

1. Campaigning

Campaigning is only allowed during the official campaign period. All campaign materials must be approved by the Election Commission. Negative campaigning and personal attacks are strictly prohibited.

2. Voting

Each student is entitled to one vote per position. Proxy voting or selling of votes is a serious offense and will result in disciplinary action.

3. Candidacy

Candidates must meet the academic and behavioral requirements set by the Student Affairs office. Failure to maintain these standards may result in disqualification.

4. Disputes

Any complaints regarding the election process must be filed formally with the Election Commission within 24 hours of the incident or result announcement.
