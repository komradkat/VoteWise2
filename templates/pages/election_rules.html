Election Rules

Guidelines for a fair and transparent election.

1. Voter Eligibility

To be eligible to vote, you must be a currently enrolled student with a valid student ID and active status for the current academic term. Students on leave of absence or suspension are not eligible to participate.

2. Candidacy Requirements

Candidates must maintain a minimum GPA of 2.5 and have no record of major disciplinary infractions. All candidates must attend the mandatory election orientation and sign the Code of Conduct.

3. Campaign Regulations

Campaigning is permitted only within designated areas and timeframes. All campaign materials (posters, social media posts) must be approved by the Election Commission. Negative campaigning, slander, and harassment of other candidates are strictly prohibited.

4. Voting Procedures

Voting is conducted electronically via the VoteWise platform. Each student is entitled to one vote per position. Any form of vote buying, coercion, or unauthorized access to another student's account is a serious violation of election integrity.

5. Violations & Penalties

Violations of these rules may result in immediate disqualification of a candidate, invalidation of votes, and referral to the Student Disciplinary Committee for further action.
