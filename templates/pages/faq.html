Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the student election process.

When is the election?

Election dates are announced via official school channels and listed on the Elections page.

Can I change my vote?

No. Once your ballot is submitted, it is final and cannot be changed. Please review your choices carefully before submitting.

Is my vote anonymous?

Yes. The system separates your identity from your ballot choices. We know that you voted, but not who you voted for.

What if I can't log in?

If you are having trouble logging in, please contact the IT support desk or visit the Student Affairs office for assistance.
