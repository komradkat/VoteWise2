Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Information Collection

We collect limited personal information necessary to verify your eligibility to vote, including your student ID, name, and enrollment status. This data is sourced from the official student database.

2. Vote Anonymity

Your vote is secret. While we track who has voted to prevent double voting, your specific ballot choices are encrypted and stored separately from your identity. It is impossible to link a specific vote back to a specific student.

3. Data Usage

Your data is used solely for the purpose of administering student elections. We do not share your personal information with third parties or use it for any other purpose.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data and the integrity of the election results, including encryption, secure session management, and access controls.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact the Student Union Office or email election@school.edu.
