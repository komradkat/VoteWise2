Voter Guide

Everything you need to know about casting your vote.

1. Check Your Eligibility

Ensure you are currently enrolled and have a valid student account. You can check your status by logging into the student portal.

2. Review Candidates

Browse the Elections page to see who is running. Read their platforms and biographies to make an informed decision.

3. Cast Your Vote

On election day, log in to VoteWise and navigate to the active election. Select your preferred candidates for each position and submit your ballot.

4. Verify Your Vote

After voting, you will receive a confirmation receipt. You can use this to verify that your vote was counted, while maintaining the secrecy of your choices.
