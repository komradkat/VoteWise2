Voter Guide

Everything you need to know about casting your vote.

1. Login & Dashboard

Log in using your student credentials. Once logged in, you will be directed to your Voter Dashboard, where you can see active elections and your voting status.

2. Know Your Candidates

Before voting, visit the Candidate Portal to review candidate profiles, platforms, and biographies. Make an informed choice!

3. Select Your Election

On the Elections List page, click "Vote Now" on the active election you wish to participate in.

4. Cast Your Vote

Select your preferred candidates for each position. You can review your selections before final submission. Once submitted, your vote is encrypted and cannot be changed.

5. Confirmation

After submitting, you will receive a digital receipt confirming your vote has been recorded. You can view your voting history on your dashboard.
